Rendang

Description

Beef Rendang is a magnificent, slow-cooked Indonesian and Malaysian dish celebrated for its complex depth of flavor and tender texture. It is a dry curry characterized by an aromatic paste known as rempah, which typically includes ingredients such as chilies, shallots, garlic, ginger, turmeric, galangal, and lemongrass. These spices are saut√©ed and then simmered with cubes of beef (traditionally tough cuts like shank or chuck) in rich coconut milk and toasted coconut flakes, or kerisik.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • coconut milk
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • galangal
  • chili
  • lemongrass

Steps

  1. Prepare the spice paste by blending aromatics and spices.
  2. Saute the paste until fragrant.
  3. Brown the beef before adding the coconut milk, whole spices, and seasonings and simmer the mixture.
  4. uncover and continue to cook on low heat for several hours, stirring frequently until the sauce thickens into rich, dark paste that coats the tender beef.
